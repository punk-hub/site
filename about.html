About Punkhub

"That's me in the street with a violin under my chin. Playin' with a grin. Singin' gibberish"

Linoleum - NoFX

Punkhub is a punk/pop punk/ska covers band based in Portsmouth, UK. We bring punk to venues of all sizes.

Playing 21st Century Pop Punk anthems from legends like Blink 182, Green Day, and Goldfinger to old school classics of Sex Pistols, The Clash, and Dead Kennedys.

Pick Punkhub! for your pub or party, you're promised a punkin good time
